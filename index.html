FAQ

  • 01. Are Bhakhri and Chapdi gluten-free

    Traditional Bhakhri made from whole wheat flour contains gluten, while variations like Bajri Bhakhri can be gluten-free. Chapdi, made from gram flour, is typically gluten-free.

  • 02. How long does Bhakhri stay fresh

    Bhakhri typically stays fresh for about 30 to 35 days when stored in an airtight container at room temperature. It is best consumed fresh for optimal taste and texture

  • 03. What are the different variations of Products?

    There are various types of Bhakhri, such as MANCHURIAN Bhakhari , Masala Bhakhari , Jeera Bhakhari, METHI Bhakhari , PERI PERI Bhakhari , GARLIC Bhakhari , MANCHURIAN Bhakhari and more .

  • 04. How are your products packaged?

    Our products are packaged in high-quality, food-grade materials to ensure they remain fresh and safe. We use airtight packaging to preserve their texture and flavor.

  • 05. Do you have any valid license for your product?

    Yes, we have obtained a valid FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) license for our products, demonstrating our commitment to meeting food safety and quality standards set by regulatory authorities.

  • 06. Do you have a loyalty program for regular customers?

    Yes, we value our regular customers and offer a loyalty program where you can earn points for every purchase. These points can be redeemed for discounts, free products, or special promotions, making your Product experience even more rewarding.
