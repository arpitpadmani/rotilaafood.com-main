About Us

About Rotilaa
Welcome to the family of Rotilaa, a place where you will find the most delicious Bhakhari ever! When cooked with the proper ingredients and recipe, Bhakhari tastes its finest. With a generations-old recipe rooted in Gujarat's rich history, Rotilla makes Bhakhari that will bring back childhood memories. Rotilaa is not just a brand but a celebration of the culinary traditions of Bhakhari. These vary from the ones made with the recipes handed down through generations to innovative variations to match our present taste palates.
Our Mission
Our goal is to take the authentic taste of Bhakhari to the palates of the largest number of people. Bhakhari is a popular dish in Gujarat, and we aim to make it reach to pan India and more. We aspire to become the custodian of the classic taste of Bhakhari mixed with modern creativity. The core mission of Rotilaa is to maintain the top-notch quality of our products.
Our vision
At Rotilaa, we envision making Bhakari a traditional food and culinary specialty that passes through boundaries and generations. We try to make Rotilaa a go-to brand for folks looking for authentic yet modern-tasting Bhakari.A trip into the heart of Bhakhari craft is what we also aim to achieve with every customer visit to Rotilaa.
